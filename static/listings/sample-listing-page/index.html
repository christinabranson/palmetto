    Description

    Step right into this beautifully well-maintained single-family home located in the heart of Bluffton, Westbury Park. This Charleston style home boasts 3 bedrooms and 3 full baths. The first floor boasts an updated kitchen with granite countertops and stainless steel Frigidaire appliances, a large bedroom with walk-in closet, and a full bathroom with stall shower. The second floor houses two master suites, both of which have access to a porch that stretches the length of the upstairs; a perfect place for that morning cup of coffee. The larger master bedroom has a large, upgraded walk-in closet and bathroom with dual sinks. Washer and Dryer are included and are also conveniently located upstairs. Located in the backyard is a large patio with off-street parking for 2+ cars. This home is maintenance-free!

    Neighborhood

    Westbury Park is a gated community with a community pool and fitness center. You couldn’t ask for a more convenient location, just off of Highway 278, near shopping and dining and just ten minutes from the Hilton Head bridge.
